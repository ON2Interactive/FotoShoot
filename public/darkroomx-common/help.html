Getting Started

New to FotoShoot? Start by creating your account on Sign Up, then open the workspace and upload your first product image.

  • Upload one or more product images
  • Clean up the shot and improve the baseline image
  • Use presets or prompts to direct the final look
  • Export polished assets for your channels

Core Workflow

FotoShoot is designed to help you move from rough source image to polished marketing asset in one focused workflow. Upload, refine, direct, crop, and export without bouncing between multiple tools.

Using prompts and presets

FotoShoot gives you two simple ways to shape the result: choose a preset or describe the direction you want. The clearer your instruction, the more predictable the output.

  • Use presets when you want a faster starting point
  • Use prompts when you want more specific art direction
  • Describe the environment, lighting, or surface you want
  • Keep instructions focused on what should change around the product
  • Try a few variations and keep the strongest result

Keeping the product accurate

FotoShoot is being built around product fidelity. The goal is to preserve the product itself while allowing changes to the lighting, background, styling, and overall presentation.

  • Keep prompts specific and commercially realistic
  • Avoid asking the model to redesign the product itself
  • Review labels, proportions, and materials before export

Refreshing the browser

FotoShoot is being set up to preserve your active workspace locally so you do not lose your current work if the page refreshes.

  • Give uploads a moment to save after major changes
  • Refresh only after edits finish processing
  • If something fails to restore, try reloading once more before starting over

    • Exporting your assets

    Once you have a result you like, export it for the channel you need. FotoShoot is intended to support ecommerce, campaigns, ads, social content, and other product-marketing outputs.

    • Review the final image at full size before export
    • Check crop safety for the placement you need
    • Confirm the product still looks accurate before sharing or publishing
