FAQs

What is FotoShoot?

FotoShoot is an AI-powered product imagery workspace that helps you turn rough product photos into polished studio, marketplace, editorial, and lifestyle-ready visuals.

What kind of images can I start with?

You can start with simple product photos, supplier images, phone photos, or existing product shots. FotoShoot is designed to help clean them up and turn them into stronger marketing assets.

Do I need a full studio setup to use FotoShoot?

No. FotoShoot is built for people who want better product imagery without the time, cost, and complexity of a full production workflow.

Can FotoShoot keep the product itself accurate?

Yes. FotoShoot is being shaped around product fidelity, which means the product should stay true while the environment, lighting, surface, and presentation around it can change.

What can I create with FotoShoot?

You can create cleaner product images for ecommerce, launches, social content, ads, campaign visuals, and other brand-ready use cases from a single source image.

Can I direct the style of the output?

Yes. FotoShoot supports preset-driven direction and prompt-based edits so you can guide the result instead of relying on one generic output.

Can I use FotoShoot for ecommerce and marketplace listings?

Yes. FotoShoot is intended for storefront, marketplace, and marketing use cases where clean, believable, commercially useful product visuals matter.

Can I use FotoShoot for client or commercial work?

Yes. FotoShoot is built for commercial imagery workflows, though final outputs should always be reviewed before publishing or delivering to clients.

Do I need to install anything?

No. FotoShoot runs in the browser, so you can work directly online without installing a traditional desktop app.

Will my work disappear if I refresh the browser?

FotoShoot is being built to keep your active workspace available locally so you do not lose your session when the page reloads.
